Donation Process

The donation process from the time you arrive center until the time you leave

Registration

You need to complete a simple registration form with contact info to start the donation process.

Screening

A drop of blood is taken to ensure your iron level is sufficient for donation.

Donation

After screening, you're guided to a donor bed. It takes just 6–10 minutes.

Refreshment

Relax and enjoy a drink before you leave — feel strong and proud!
